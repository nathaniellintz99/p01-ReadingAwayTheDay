Reading Away the Day: Knights and Dragons

"AGH! Fuck you dragon! You're dead to me!". The dragon lets out a typical dragon ROARRR and flys toward you.

You realize your mistake. Your sword is useless to an airborn dragon. You hopelessly throw the sword at the oncoming threat; you miss. In response, the dragon opens its mouth again and spits a fireball at you. Right before you meet your end a bright light blinds you and the scene changes.

You're back in your room, slouched in your beanbag chair.
"That senario would have ended better if I had chosen the crossbow" you think to yourself.
