Reading Away the Day: Knights and Dragons

You pick up the sword and proceed through the tunnel to the arena. Once the arena comes into sight you understand the difficulty of your task.

You're in a massive cage that spans over a few thousand feet. There are some small rocky mountains, a small forrest of trees below and in between all of it in a giant nest the threat sleeps. The dragon is a dark brown-gold color with thick armor covering nearly every inch of its skin. After studying it's full length the weaknesses you spot are its exposed eyes and under belly.

  1. Go up mountain for high ground
  2. Go stealthily through forrest to get closer
  3. Yell profanity at dragon because dragons suck
