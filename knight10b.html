Reading Away the Day: Knights and Dragons

"AGH! Fuck you dragon! You're dead to me!". The dragon lets out a typical dragon ROARRR and flys toward you.

You wind back the crossbow and aim sights. The dragon soars right above you and *click* the arrow flys straight into the dragon's exposed under belly. It lets out cry and crashes downward, slain.

Before you have time to celebrate your victory the scene changes back to your familiar room. "That was a piece of cake!". You happily set the book back on the bookshelf.
