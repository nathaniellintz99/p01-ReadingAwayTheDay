Reading Away the Day: Knights and Dragons

You retreat to your nook and sit down in your cozy beanbag chair. You open up the book to the halfway mark, so you won't know what's going on. You grin "That's half the fun!". You shove your face into the pages and WOOSH! The lights go out and you can't see a thing. BAM! A bright light blind you then goes away and reveals your in small stone room leading toward a tunnel. There's natural light shining from the tunnel- that must be the way to the dragon.

Blocking your way to the tunnel is a servant holding out a sword and crossbow.

"Sir Jon, choose your weapon that you will use to slay the dragon. Then you may enter the arena." says the squire.
