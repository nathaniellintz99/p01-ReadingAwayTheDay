Reading Away the Day: Knights and Dragons

You aim your crossbow at the dragon's eye. The dragon's eye is shut, but if you hit it dead center it will pierce the eyelid.

You fire and... you miss by a foot. It bounces off the dragons thick armor. In response, the now awake dragon glares at you and spits fire at you.

Before you can scream in terror the scene changes before your eyes and you're back in your familiar bedroom.

"Hmm... maybe I should have waited to use the crossbow on the dragon's under belly. That's a much larger target!"
