Reading Away the Day: War

You reach the Bunker where the men are trapped. "Hey! Can you hear me?!" you shout through the Bunker's busted door.
"Yes! Help us get out, we can't seem to open the door. There's 5 of us trapped in here!"
"Stand back!" yells Briggs. With all his brute strength he kicks the door down. The trapped men come out one by one. You can see the exhaustion in their eyes.
"Alright boys, were getting you out of here. Follow me North-West away from the battle" commands Briggs. You take the rear and make your way back to where you started.

Suddenly, an enemy mortar lands several feet to your right. You have a split second to react.

Find cover and save yourself or jump on the mortar and save your fellow soldiers?
