Reading Away the Day: War

"Get Cover!" you scream as you dive into a small hole and cover your ears.

BOOM! Fire and shrapnel fly above you. Then silence.

You peep out of your whole and fire and smoke blinds you. Your heart sinks; it looks like no one else survived the explosion.
"Anyone there!?" you yell. A few seconds pass by, then-
"Here!". One of the soldiers from the bunker survived.
"Shit! We're the only ones that made it." You both take a moment of silence for your fallen soldiers.
"Okay, let's get out of here before another mortar comes our way!"

It's been hours since you left the warzone and you still haven't reached camp. It's getting dark and you realize you can end the story with the gun you're carrying.

Turn East and look for your camp or give up hope with your gun?
