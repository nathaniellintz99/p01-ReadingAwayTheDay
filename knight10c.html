Reading Away the Day: Knights and Dragons

You slowly close the gap between you and the dragon.

Crack! you stepped on a tree branch and broke the silence. The dragon growls and and stirs.

You sprint and reach your target. "AHGG!" you cry out as you drive your sword into the belly of the beast.

Victory! You celebrate as the scene changes before your eyes and you're back in your familiar bedroom.
