Reading Away the Day: War

You jump toward the mortar and pull it close to your chest. BOOM! Pain overcomes you for just an instant then a bright light follows.

"Agh!" you quickly sit up, heart racing. On your lap is the War story, open as you left it. You're back in your familar beanbag chair and your safe home.

"It hurt for an instant... but that was cool being the hero" you think to yourself as you close the book.
