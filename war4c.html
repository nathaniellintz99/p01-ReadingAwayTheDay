Reading Away the Day: War

You raise your machine gun and line the sights. You gently squeeze the trigger. The Nazi soldier falls.

"Hell Yeah!" you rejoice.

You realize your mistake before it's too late. The gunshot broke the silence and alerted a small group of Nazi soldiers further up the hill. You sprint down the hill but are shot almost instantly. You hit the ground and BAM! your suddenly back in your room.

"Geeze I always forget about that group of soldiers!". You sigh and close the book.
